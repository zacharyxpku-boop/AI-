# Wenai Current Product Status

## Current Shape

Wenai is no longer a single AI generation demo. It is an ecommerce AI content industrialization workbench built around:

- `Compose`: creative intelligence, competitor accounts, trend/rank signals, video teardown, hook bank, brand learning, action queue
- `Create`: asset library, scripts, production handoff, governed deliverables
- `Cut`: video workflow, smart-remix queue, provider gate, result ingestion, client review
- `Cast`: distribution plans, account matrix, dispatch ledger, campaign ledger, performance return
- `Manage`: readiness, CRM handoff, client review, RBAC/audit, asset permissions, performance learning

The product is currently a verified internal full-chain skeleton with operational gates. It is not yet a Kuaizi-level live platform executor because real external providers and platform grants are still missing.

## What Works Internally

- `/status`: readiness, final product command center, competitor gaps, external stop lines
- `/factory/video`: video production passport for AI video / smart remix / one-click-video claims
- `/settings/kuaizi`: external integration material packs and acquisition paths
- `/review/[token]`: Clico-style customer review entry
- `/api/creative-intelligence`: competitor and creative intelligence ledger
- `/api/creative-monitoring`: source/watchlist tasks and provider-gated collection
- `/api/industrial-chain/*`: assets, production handoff, results, dispatch, CRM handoff, review
- `/api/asset-permissions`: RBAC, access audit, storage object and enforcement model
- `/api/brand-learning-profile`: hook, pacing, winning assets, avoid patterns and next-round rules

## Competitive References

- Kuaizi: Compose / Create / Cut / Cast / Manage, matrix distribution, ads, enterprise asset/security, scale operations
- Hookshot / Hookly: hook extraction, UGC ad structures, governed agent execution
- Creatify: product-to-UGC-video ad creation
- Omneky: creative generation, campaign launch and performance learning
- Marpipe: catalog/SKU creative testing
- Pencil: generative ad creative plus brand memory
- AdHawk: governed media buying automation

## Gaps

Severe:
- Real video generation/editing provider execution and callback
- Real multi-platform OAuth and account grants
- Auto publish / PubPal-style matrix distribution receipts
- Ad account authorization and campaign API
- Platform analytics sync

Medium:
- Always-on creative source collection and multimodal video parsing
- Enterprise object storage, signed URL enforcement and team cloud space
- Non-technical customer frontstage polish
- Audited Wenai-owned scale ledger

## External Materials Needed

- Ad account/campaign pack

P1:

- Audited scale ledger pack

See `docs/EXTERNAL_INTEGRATION_MATERIALS.md` and `/settings/kuaizi` for where to obtain each material, how to provide it safely, and what evidence unlocks each gate.

## Stop Lines

- No provider callback: do not claim one-click finished video or batch smart remixing.
- No platform OAuth: keep distribution as manual/provider-gated dispatch.
- No ad account authorization: do not claim automatic ad delivery or optimization.
- No analytics sync: do not claim automatic performance learning beyond manual import.
- No object storage and signed URLs: do not claim enterprise cloud asset enforcement.
- No audited scale ledger: do not display Wenai-owned `91M+ creative output` or `42M+ video distribution` claims.

## Commercial Readiness Judgment

Wenai is ready as a partner-reviewable commercial POC core. It should not be sold as a Kuaizi-equivalent platform until the P0 external materials are configured and verified with real callback, account, campaign and performance evidence.
